# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Technical Writer

### Role Summary
Technical Writers maintain and evolve project documentation to ensure knowledge is accessible, accurate, and up to date. They serve as the connective tissue between technical decisions and the broader team by translating complexity into clear, actionable content.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain and update project documentation
- Ensure information is accessible, versioned, and searchable
- Clarify technical decisions and rationale for future reference
- Create user guides, API documentation, and onboarding materials

### Goals
- Reduce knowledge silos and single-person dependencies
- Accelerate onboarding for new team members
- Maintain living documentation that evolves with the product

### Typical Communication
- Collaborates with PM, Developers, and QA to keep docs current
- Supports onboarding with clear, comprehensive artifacts
- Works with Product Manager to document feature specifications

---

## Change Manager

### Role Summary
Change Managers oversee process and scope changes throughout the project lifecycle. They ensure that changes are tracked, assessed for impact, and communicated transparently to all stakeholders.

### Responsibilities
- Oversee process and scope changes throughout project lifecycle
- Maintain change log and impact assessments
- Communicate changes and approvals to stakeholders
- Facilitate change review boards when needed

### Goals
- Minimize disruption from uncontrolled changes
- Ensure transparent change tracking and approval processes
- Balance agility with stability

### Typical Communication
- Works closely with PM and Product Manager to facilitate stakeholder alignment
- Conducts impact analysis with technical leads
- Coordinates with Release Manager on deployment changes

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers advocate for usability and customer experience throughout the product lifecycle. They translate user research and business requirements into intuitive, accessible designs that guide implementation.

### Responsibilities
- Advocate for usability and customer experience
- Design user flows, wireframes, and prototypes
- Ensure customer-centric design decisions
- Conduct user research and usability testing

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce customer friction and support burden
- Validate designs with real user feedback

### Typical Communication
- Collaborates with Product Manager on feature requirements
- Works with Developers to validate UX throughout implementation
- Partners with QA to ensure acceptance criteria include usability

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads develop test strategies, coordinate quality assurance activities, and advocate for quality gates across the delivery lifecycle. They ensure acceptance criteria are testable and met before release.

### Responsibilities
- Develop test strategies and coordinate QA activities
- Ensure acceptance criteria are testable and met
- Track defect trends and quality metrics
- Advocate for quality gates and testing best practices

### Goals
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Maintain high quality standards and test coverage
- Reduce production incidents through comprehensive testing

### Typical Communication
- Works with Developers on test automation and coverage
- Partners with Product Manager to validate acceptance criteria
- Reports quality risks and blockers to PM
- Coordinates with Release Manager on pre-release testing

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge the gap between business stakeholders and the delivery team. They gather, clarify, and document requirements to ensure technical solutions align with business objectives.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements
- Translate business needs into technical tasks and user stories
- Validate that solutions meet business objectives
- Facilitate requirements workshops and stakeholder interviews

### Goals
- Ensure clear, unambiguous requirements
- Reduce rework from misunderstood requirements
- Align technical solutions with business value

### Typical Communication
- Acts as bridge between stakeholders, PM, and Developers
- Works with Product Manager on prioritization and backlog refinement
- Supports Technical Writer in documenting business processes
- Clarifies scope and requirements throughout delivery
